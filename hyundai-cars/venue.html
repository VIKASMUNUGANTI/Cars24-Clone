About Creta

The Hyundai Verna is one of the most stylish and performance-oriented sedans in its segment, designed to deliver a perfect balance of elegance, technology, and driving excitement. With its bold and futuristic design language, the Verna stands out through its sharp LED headlamps, signature connected DRLs, and aerodynamic profile that enhances both aesthetics and efficiency. Inside, the cabin offers a premium experience with high-quality materials, a dual-tone finish, and a wide integrated digital display that combines the infotainment system and instrument cluster. The car is equipped with advanced features such as a large touchscreen with wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, ventilated front seats, a premium sound system, ambient lighting, and a sunroof, making every drive comfortable and engaging.
